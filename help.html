Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I create an account?

To create an account, simply click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in the required details. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

How can I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page, and we'll send you instructions on how to reset it.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact our support team by using the contact form on the "Contact" page. We'll respond to your inquiry as soon as possible.
